Piotr Rasputin, the Artist

Born to a simple farm life in Russia, Piotr Rasputin was the younger brother of the famous cosmonaut Mikhail Rasputin.

Piotr has been using his mutant power to turn to organic steel, to fight for the peaceful coexistence of mutants and humans most of his life.

After his younger sister Illyana Rasputin died of the Legacy Virus, Piotr began to lose resolve and joined Magnetos Acolytes on Avalon.
